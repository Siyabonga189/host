How to Use LearnMateHub

Welcome to LearnMateHub, a platform designed to help you connect with peer tutors and enhance your academic experience. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Search for a Tutor:
    • Click on the "Find a Tutor" section.
    • Use the filters to specify the subject, location, and availability you need.
    • View the list of available tutors and click "View Profile" to learn more about each tutor.
  2. Schedule a Tutoring Session:
    • Click on the "Schedule a Tutoring Session" section.
    • Choose a suitable tutor and available time slot.
    • Click "Book Now" to confirm your tutoring session.
  3. Access Online Tutoring:
    • When it's time for your session, visit the "Online Tutoring Platform" section.
    • Click on the provided link to join the online session.
    • Use video conferencing, the collaborative whiteboard, and chat to interact with your tutor.
  4. Leave Ratings and Reviews:
    • After your tutoring session, go to the "Ratings and Reviews" section.
    • Leave a rating and review for your tutor to help build a trustworthy community.
  5. Engage in the Discussion Board:
    • Check out the "Discussion Board" section for peer support and dynamic learning.
    • Participate in discussions and ask questions to the community.
